# RevHunter AI Sales Engine 🚀

**The Relentless Multi-Tenant Automotive Lead Generation & Management System**

RevHunter AI is a high-performance, white-label Full-Stack AI solution designed for automotive dealerships. It enables agencies to provide AI-driven sales automation to multiple dealerships from a single platform, automating the entire lead lifecycle—from social media engagement to CRM integration.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🏢 Multi-Tenant Architecture

- **White-Labeling**: Easily rebrand the entire platform for different dealerships with a simple configuration.
- **Tenant-Specific Logic**: Each dealership gets its own AI personality, welcome messages, and inventory-specific handling.
- **Centralized Admin**: Manage multiple dealerships from one unified "RevHunter AI" dashboard.

### 🤖 The RevHunter AI Roles

- **Receptionist**: 24/7 Intelligent Greeting and initial lifestyle discovery.
- **Sales Agent**: A relentless 9-step sales script following automotive best practices (Lifestyle, Trade-ins, Financing).
- **Marketing Manager**: AI-generated ad copy and social posts with a **Human-in-the-Loop** approval workflow.

### 🏹 Relentless Hunter Capabilities

- **Dead-Lead Miner**: Automated reactivation of aged leads using intelligent follow-up.
- **Accountability Audit Log**: Transparent, time-stamped tracking of all AI and Human actions.
- **Daily Quality Reporting**: Automated generation of top-tier lead reports with projected ROI.
